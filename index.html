Nursing Bras:

 

Nursing bras are specifically designed for breastfeeding mothers. Featuring clasps for easy access, they also include added support and comfort for her changing breast size. Victoria's Secret offers wireless, padded, and other bra styles that accommodate for nursing mothers’ needs.

 

Strapless Bras:

 

Strapless bras are a must-have for any wardrobe. Ideal for looks like off-the-shoulder dresses and open-back tops, Victoria's Secret has a variety of strapless, backless, and other no-show bra solutions that provide support while staying stylish.

 

Supportive Sports Bras:

 

Sports bras are designed to provide support during physical activity and comfort during workouts. Victoria’s Secret offers options ranging from low-to-high impact in styles like wire-free, padded, and more—that all look great with your activewear wardrobe.

 

Push-Up Bras:

 

Push-up bras are designed to enhance your shape and create a natural lift. With the help of supportive padding, Victoria's Secret’s push-up bras will boost your look and your confidence.

 

Romantic Lingerie :

 

Lingerie is for feeling sensual and confident. From lacy and elegant Babydolls to playful and seductive Bodysuits, Victoria’s Secret’s selection has a look for every occasion.

 

Comfortable Wireless Bras :

 

For women who prefer a more natural feel, wireless bras provide support without the use of underwires. Victoria's Secret offers a variety of wire-free bras, from padded to lace, that are ideal for everyday comfort..
